Who am I?

Hello! My name is Kasra Azizi, I'm a dedicated Robotics Researcher and enthusiast with a strong foundation in Mechatronics Engineering, specializing in control systems. My journey into robotics has been fueled by an unwavering passion for the intersection of mechanics, electronics, and computer science—an ever-evolving field that promises to reshape the future of technology and automation.

From an early age, I was fascinated by the complexities of machines and the limitless potential they hold, inspiring me to dive deeper into the captivating world of robotics. My academic and research experiences have further fueled this passion, helping me navigate the challenges of innovation and breakthrough. Motivated by the endless possibilities in robotics, I am dedicated to contributing to advancements that will revolutionize industries and enhance the quality of human life.

Research Interests

  • Autonomous Systems

    Focusing on the development of intelligent systems capable of independent decision-making and operation.
